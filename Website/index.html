Star Wars Project framework

A proprietary ontology framwork assembling all the important entities, relationships and attributes to make the star wars triple store. Not to be used anywhere else, scope of this website extends to the project work ONLY.

Important Information

This work is compiled with the intention of providing supporting information and proprietary data about the Star Wars triple store.

Basic Class Defintions

ex:Jedi

A member of the Jedi Order, dedicated to the principles of the Force.

ex:Grandmaster
A Subclass of the Jedi class

The highest-ranking Jedi, responsible for leading the Jedi Order.

ex:JediKnight
A Subclass of the Jedi class

A fully trained Jedi who has completed their apprenticeship and is capable of undertaking independent missions.

ex:JediMaster
A Subclass of the Jedi class

A Jedi who has demonstrated exceptional skill and wisdom, often responsible for training Padawans.

ex:JediCouncilMember
A Subclass of the Jedi class

A Jedi who serves on the Jedi High Council, involved in making important decisions for the Jedi Order.

ex:TrainingProgram

A structured program designed to train individuals in various Jedi skills and knowledge.

ex:GroundVehicle
A Subclass of the schema.org Type Vehicle class

A vehicle designed for travel on the ground, including speeder bikes and walkers.

ex:Starship
A Subclass of the schema.org Type Vehicle class

A spacecraft designed for travel in space, including starfighters and cruisers.

ex:Planet

A celestial body that orbits a star and can support various forms of life.

ex:TempleInfrastructure

Buildings and structures associated with the Jedi Temple, used for various purposes including training and governance.

ex:Mission

A specific task or operation undertaken by Jedi or other entities, often involving combat or diplomacy.

ex:Artifact

An object of historical or cultural significance, often imbued with the Force or representing important Jedi heritage.
